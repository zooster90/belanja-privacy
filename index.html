Belanja Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026 | Effective Date: April 8, 2026

Key Principle: Belanja is an offline-first personal finance app. Your financial data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal financial information on our servers.

1. Who We Are

Belanja ("the App") is developed and published by Belanja ("we", "us", "our"). The App is a personal finance tracker designed for individuals in Malaysia and worldwide to manage expenses, track budgets, scan receipts, and optimize tax deductions.

Package ID: com.solocfo.user

4. Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud backup:

  • Your data is backed up to your own Google Drive account using your Google credentials.
  • We do not have access to your Google Drive. The backup file is stored in your personal Drive storage.
  • Backup files are encrypted ZIP archives containing your Hive database and receipt images.
  • You can delete backups at any time from your Google Drive.

5. Advertising

  • Free-tier users see banner advertisements powered by Google AdMob on secondary screens (history, settings).
  • Ads are never shown during core flows (adding expenses, viewing dashboard).
  • Premium users (via in-app purchase) see no advertisements.
  • We do not use your financial data to target advertisements. Ad targeting is handled entirely by Google AdMob based on their own policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

Your data may only be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Receipt images and anonymized spending summaries are sent to Groq API servers for processing. This data is processed in real-time and not retained by Groq.
  • Crash Reporting: Anonymous crash logs and device diagnostics are sent to Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.
  • Advertising: Google AdMob may collect device identifiers as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Malaysian law or valid legal process.

7. Data Retention

  • On-device data: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud backups: Retained in your Google Drive until you delete them.
  • Crash logs: Retained by Firebase Crashlytics for 90 days.
  • AI processing: Not retained. Processed in real-time only.

8. Your Rights

Under Malaysia's Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) and applicable international privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: View all your data directly in the app (it's all local).
  • Export: Export all transactions as CSV or PDF from Settings.
  • Delete: Clear all data from Settings > Data & Security > Clear All Data.
  • Portability: Create a full backup (ZIP) from Settings > Backup.
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable optional features (ads tracking, cloud sync, notifications) at any time.

9. Account Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Go to Settings > Data & Security > Clear All Data
  • Confirm the deletion. This permanently removes all transactions, accounts, goals, budgets, categories, and receipt images from your device.
  • If you used Google Drive backup, manually delete the backup files from your Drive.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all remaining cached data.

10. Children's Privacy

Belanja is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided data through the app, please contact us and we will assist with deletion.

11. Security

  • All local data is stored using Hive encrypted boxes with AES-256 encryption.
  • Sensitive credentials (biometric settings) use Flutter Secure Storage (Android Keystore / iOS Keychain).
  • Optional biometric lock (fingerprint/face) prevents unauthorized app access.
  • Network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
